# เรื่องที่ 47: Networking (สร้างเครือข่าย)

## 📖 เรื่องสั้น (3 ภาษา)

**Networking**

Ploy is a young professional. She wants to advance her career.

"Networking is important. I need to meet people," Ploy says.

Ploy attends industry events. She brings business cards.

"Hi, I'm Ploy. I work in marketing," she says.

She asks questions. She listens more than she talks.

"What do you do? What projects are you working on?" Ploy asks.

Ploy connects on LinkedIn. She follows up with new contacts.

"Great meeting you. Let's stay in touch," she says.

Ploy joins professional associations. She volunteers for committees.

"I meet senior professionals. I learn from them," Ploy says.

She attends conferences. She participates in discussions.

After 1 year, Ploy has 500+ LinkedIn connections.

A contact refers her to a job opening.

"There's a position at my company. You'd be perfect," he says.

Ploy applies. She gets the job.

"Networking opened doors for me," Ploy says.

Her new salary is 40% higher.

"Your network is your net worth," Ploy says.

She continues networking. It's an ongoing investment.
