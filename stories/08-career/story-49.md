# เรื่องที่ 49: Overcoming Failure (ก้าวข้ามความล้มเหลว)

## 📖 เรื่องสั้น (3 ภาษา)

**Overcoming Failure**

Tum started a business. It failed after 1 year.

"I lost 500,000 baht. I'm devastated," Tum says.

Tum is depressed. He doesn't want to try again.

His friend encourages him. "Failure is a lesson," the friend says.

Tum analyzes what went wrong.

"I didn't research the market. I spent too much," Tum says.

Tum gets a job. He saves money and learns.

After 2 years, Tum is ready to try again.

"I won't make the same mistakes," Tum says.

Tum starts a new business. This time, he's careful.

He validates the idea first. He starts small.

The new business grows slowly but steadily.

After 1 year, Tum's business is profitable.

"Failure taught me valuable lessons," Tum says.

After 3 years, Tum's business earns 200,000 baht per month.

"I'm grateful for my failure. It made me stronger," Tum says.

Tum mentors other entrepreneurs. He shares his lessons.

"Failure is not the end. It's a stepping stone to success," Tum says.
