# เรื่องที่ 44: Dealing with Difficult Boss (จัดการกับเจ้านายที่ยาก)

## 📖 เรื่องสั้น (2 ภาษา)

**Dealing with Difficult Boss**

Nok has a difficult boss. He's always angry.

"My boss yells at me. I'm stressed every day," Nok says.

Nok thinks about quitting. But she needs the job.

"I need to find a way to work with him," Nok says.

Nok observes her boss. She notices patterns.

"He's angry in the morning. He's calmer in the afternoon," Nok says.

Nok adjusts her approach. She avoids him in the morning.

She prepares detailed reports. She anticipates his questions.

Nok stays calm when he yells. She doesn't take it personally.

"I understand he has pressure from above," Nok says.

After 3 months, her boss notices her professionalism.

"You handle stress well. I appreciate that," he says.

Their relationship improves. He yells less.

Nok learns to set boundaries. "I won't accept disrespect," she says.

After 1 year, her boss respects her.

"You're one of my best employees," he says.

Nok got a raise. She's happier at work.

"Difficult people teach you patience and professionalism," Nok says.
