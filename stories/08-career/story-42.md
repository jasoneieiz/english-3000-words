# เรื่องที่ 42: Teamwork (การทำงานเป็นทีม)

## 📖 เรื่องสั้น (3 ภาษา)

**Teamwork**

The company has a big project. They need a team to complete it.

"Teamwork is essential for this project," the boss says.

Five people are selected: Somchai, Nida, Krit, Pim, and Tom.

Somchai is the team leader. He assigns tasks.

"Let's play to our strengths," Somchai says.

Nida is good at design. Krit is good at coding.

Pim handles marketing. Tom does quality testing.

Somchai coordinates everything.

At first, there are problems. "My part isn't ready yet," Krit says.

The team meets. They discuss the issues.

"We need to communicate better," Nida says.

They set up daily check-in meetings.

They use Slack for quick communication.

After 2 weeks, the team works smoothly.

"I learned that teamwork makes the dream work," Somchai says.

The project is completed on time. The client is happy.

"Great job, team! Let's celebrate!" the boss says.

They go for dinner together. They're proud of their work.

"Teamwork is the ability to work together toward a common goal," Somchai says.
